About Me

Got that? Eh, don't worry. I hear it mispronounced all the time and I'm fairin' just fine.

Professional Emotional Support Critter

When I'm trying to actually get paid for things, I am a licensed pastor in The United Methodist Church. I currently serve as a resident chaplain at St. Anthony's Hospital North. A chaplain is kind of like a therapy animal, except that we can talk (and ask great questions for self-reflection!) and you can't pet us.

I am also faculty with Creating a Culture of Renewal, a ministry leadership development program. I am also the cofounder of Fearless Future, a holistic self-defense program that empowers people in body, mind, and soul, designed with trans- and gender-expansive folx in mind. I also work as a merchandiser, but you really don't need to hear more about that.
